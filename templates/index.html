How it Works

This is a simple Kanban where you can add and move cards between 4 steps, to use by your own project, you just need to put an project after the url, for example: https://cardsgo.ddns.net/cards?project=. Of course, this site is public, so another person can access your annotaions, so, don't put any sensitive data on it.
